About Me

I am a Ph.D student supervised by Prof. Carlo A Furia at the Software Institute, USI Lugano. Previously, i was a Doctoral Research Assistant at the Institute of Information Security, ETH Zurich from 2015-2018. I have an Integrated Bachelors and Masters degree in Mathematics and Computing from IIT Kanpur, where my Masters Thesis was advised by Prof. Mohua Banerjee .

My areas of interest are in the intersection of formal methods and software engineering with focus on automated program repair. I have also worked on projects aiming to solve scalability issues for runtime monitoring on large and high velocity data traces, and explanation of counterexamples generated by model checkers.
